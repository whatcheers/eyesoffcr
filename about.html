About EyesOffCR.org

Working to protect privacy and civil liberties in Cedar Rapids

Our Mission

EyesOffCR.org is a community initiative dedicated to tracking and resisting the expansion of mass surveillance in Cedar Rapids. We believe in transparency, accountability, and the protection of civil liberties.

Our work focuses on documenting the deployment of Automated License Plate Readers (ALPRs) and advocating for proper oversight and transparency in their use.
