Holding Cedar Rapids Accountable for Automated License Plate Readers (ALPR)

March 1, 2024

Welcome to EyesOffCedarRapids, a web-based, community-driven initiative focused on transparency, accountability, and raising awareness about the Cedar Rapids Police Department's (CRPD) use of automated license plate readers (ALPR). Our mission is to spark conversations, educate the public, and ensure responsible use of this technology.

What's the Project About?

Automated license plate readers (ALPR) are powerful tools used to track vehicles in real time. While they can enhance public safety, their use raises critical questions about privacy, civil rights, and oversight.

At EyesOffCedarRapids, we aim to:

  • Raise awareness about ALPR in Cedar Rapids
  • Advocate for transparency and public involvement in its use
  • Hold CRPD accountable through community dialogue on privacy and civil liberties

What Have We Accomplished?
